What is Macadamia?

Macadamia nuts are rich, buttery nuts that are native to Australia but are also grown in various other countries. They are renowned for their high nutritional value, offering a good source of healthy fats, vitamins, and minerals. Macadamias are often used in a variety of culinary dishes, including baked goods, desserts, and as a crunchy addition to salads and other meals.
